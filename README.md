# Eye-Tracking
Eye-Tracking App 


Instructions:

1- First of all, upload image from the left-bottom section "Upload Image",

2- Secondly, Calibrate your eye-tracking by clicking "CALIBRATE" button in the section of "EYE TRACKING",

3- Then, when the calibration is done, you should press "RECORD" otherwise it won't visualise your data.

4- After you done your eye-tracking, click on "STOP" button this will stop eye-tracking and allow you to watch video with heat-map on it.

NOTE: if the image on the video doesn't show up at first don't worry, just rewind the video to the very beginning. It wil fix on its own.

5- Finally, after you pressed "STOP", you can download your .csv files by clicking "DOWNLOAD" button at the left-bottom.
